# Privacy Policy for Curate

**Last Updated: January 14, 2026**

## Introduction

Curate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Curate mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you create an account using Google Sign-In, we collect:
- **Email address**: Used for authentication and account identification
- **Display name**: Used for personalization within the app
- **Google account ID**: Used for authentication purposes

### App Activity Data

We collect and store information about your use of the App, including:
- **Media items**: Movies, TV shows, video games, and books you add to your lists
- **Ratings**: Your ratings and comparisons of media items
- **Preferences**: App settings and customization choices

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device information**: Basic device information for app functionality
- **Usage data**: Information about how you interact with the App

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Authentication**: To verify your identity and provide secure access to your account
- **Cloud Sync**: To synchronize your data across multiple devices
- **App Functionality**: To provide core features such as media rating, list management, and statistics
- **Improvement**: To improve the App's features and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
Your data is stored locally on your device using encrypted SQLite databases.

### Cloud Storage
When you sign in with Google, your data is synchronized to Firebase Firestore, which:
- Encrypts data in transit using HTTPS
- Encrypts data at rest
- Is secured according to Google's security practices

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- Secure authentication through Firebase Authentication
- Encrypted data transmission
- Secure API communications with third-party services

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

### Firebase Services (Google)
- **Firebase Authentication**: For user authentication
- **Cloud Firestore**: For cloud data synchronization

Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### The Movie Database (TMDB)
- Used to fetch movie and TV show information
- Privacy Policy: https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy

### RAWG API
- Used to fetch video game information
- Privacy Policy: https://rawg.io/privacy

### Google Books API
- Used to fetch book information
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Open Library
- Used as a backup source for book information
- Privacy Policy: https://openlibrary.org/privacy

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:
- As required by law or legal process
- With the third-party services listed above, solely for providing App functionality
- With your explicit consent

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Access
You can access all your data through the App interface.

### Deletion
You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by:
- Contacting us at m.e.berkowitz@gmail.com
- Deleting your account through the App's settings

Upon deletion request, we will:
- Delete all personal information from our servers within 30 days
- Note that some data may be retained in backups for up to 90 days

### Data Portability
You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at m.e.berkowitz@gmail.com.

### Opt-Out of Cloud Sync
You can use the App without signing in, which will keep all data stored locally on your device only.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active and for 90 days after account deletion
- **Local Data**: Retained on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data
- **Cloud Data**: Retained in Firestore until you delete your account

## Offline Use

The App can be used without an internet connection. When offline:
- Data is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to our servers or third parties
- Data will sync when you reconnect (if you're signed in)

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
